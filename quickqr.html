Privacy Policy

QuickQR

Fast QR code scanner and generator with barcode reader. Works offline, truly private with no tracking.

Welcome to QuickQR ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your choices. QuickQR runs fully on-device and does not transmit your personal data to our servers.

By using QuickQR, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the application.

Information the App Accesses

On-device Preferences (stored locally):

We store your settings locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences, including:

  • Scan history (QR codes and barcodes you've scanned)
  • Generated QR codes history
  • Favorites and bookmarked codes
  • App preferences (theme, language, etc.)
  • Encrypted QR code passwords (stored securely on-device)
  • Onboarding status and feature toggles

Camera Access (on-device only):

QuickQR requires camera permission to scan QR codes and barcodes. The camera feed is processed in real-time on your device and is never stored, recorded, or transmitted. No images or video are saved from the camera.

Flashlight Control (on-device only):

The app may control your device's flashlight to help scan codes in low-light conditions. This is done locally and no data is collected.

We do NOT collect or transmit:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, photos, videos, or files
  • Location data (GPS or network-based)
  • App usage analytics or crash reports
  • Financial information or payment data
  • Screen content or screenshots
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data to third-party services or analytics platforms

How We Use Information

  • QR Code Scanning: Process QR codes and barcodes using your device's camera in real-time.
  • QR Code Generation: Create QR codes for websites, Wi-Fi, contacts, text, and encrypted content.
  • History Management: Store scan and generation history locally for quick access.
  • Favorites: Save frequently used codes for easy retrieval.
  • Encryption: Generate password-protected QR codes that can only be decrypted with the correct password.
  • Offline Operation: All scanning and generation works completely offline without internet connection.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage:

Your scan history, generated codes, favorites, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences and local database. We do not operate any backend that stores your personal data. If your device backup is enabled, the operating system may include app data in device backups per system settings.

Encryption:

When you create password-protected QR codes, the encryption is performed on your device. The password is stored securely using Android's Keystore system. Encrypted content can only be decrypted with the correct password.

Security:

Android provides app sandboxing and OS-level protections. QuickQR does not implement additional encryption for SharedPreferences because it does not store sensitive personal data beyond what you choose to include in QR codes. Always keep your device updated and protected.

Permissions We Request and Why

  • CAMERA: To scan QR codes and barcodes using your device's camera. Required for core functionality.
  • FLASHLIGHT / TORCH: To control the flashlight for better scanning in low-light conditions (optional feature).
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+): To show notifications when needed (optional but recommended).

Permissions are used solely to deliver core functionality. We do not use them for tracking or advertising.

Third-Party Services

QuickQR does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or crash reporting SDKs. Distribution and updates are provided by Google Play. The app works completely offline and does not communicate with any external servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. We do not transmit any data to external servers. All data remains on your device. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Your Choices

  • Clear History: Delete scan history and generated codes at any time from within the app.
  • Settings: Change any preference within the app at any time.
  • Permissions: Grant or revoke runtime permissions in Android settings.
  • Clear data: Clear app data in system settings to reset to defaults.
  • Uninstall: Remove the app to delete all local data.

Children's Privacy

QuickQR is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Users

QuickQR is operated from India. The app processes data locally on your device and does not transmit your personal data to our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Significant changes will be reflected by updating the date below and, where appropriate, via an in-app notice.
