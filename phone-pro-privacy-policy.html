Privacy Policy for Phone Pro

Effective Date: January 15, 2025
Last Updated: January 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Mentiq Labs ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Phone Pro mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Contact Information

Phone Pro accesses your device's contact list to provide dialer and contact management functionality. This includes:

  • Contact names and phone numbers
  • Contact photos (if available)
  • Email addresses and other contact details
  • Organization information (company, job title)
  • Notes associated with contacts

1.2 Call Information

To provide call management features, we access:

  • Call history (incoming, outgoing, missed calls)
  • Phone numbers dialed and received
  • Call duration and timestamps
  • Phone state information

1.3 App Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app, including:

  • App performance metrics
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports and error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide phone dialer and contact management functionality
  • Enable call history tracking and management
  • Support T9 search and contact filtering features
  • Maintain favorites and contact organization
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support when requested

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your contact and call data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this information to external servers or third parties.

3.2 Data Encryption

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Local data encryption where applicable
  • Secure data transmission protocols
  • Regular security updates and patches

4. Permissions We Request

Phone Pro requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • READ_CONTACTS: To display and manage your contact list
  • WRITE_CONTACTS: To add, edit, or delete contacts
  • CALL_PHONE: To initiate phone calls
  • READ_CALL_LOG: To display call history
  • READ_PHONE_STATE: To detect incoming calls
  • CAMERA: To capture contact photos
  • RECORD_AUDIO: For voice-related features
  • READ_SMS/SEND_SMS: For SMS integration features

5. Third-Party Services

Phone Pro does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We do not share your personal information with any third parties.

6. Data Retention

Your contact and call data is retained on your device for as long as you have the app installed. You can delete this data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app
  • Clearing app data through device settings
  • Using the app's built-in data management features

7. Children's Privacy

Phone Pro is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored by the app
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data in a portable format

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
