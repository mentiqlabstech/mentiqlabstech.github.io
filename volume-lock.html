Privacy Policy

Volume Lock

Full control over your device's volume with parental controls. Lock sound levels to prevent accidental changes.

Volume Lock ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Local Data Only

Volume Lock operates entirely on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data remains on your device.

Data Stored Locally

The following information is stored locally on your device:

1. Volume Settings

  • Current volume levels for different audio streams (Media, Notification, Alarm, Ringtone, Voice Call)
  • Volume lock preferences (fixed locks, range locks)
  • Minimum and maximum range values for volume controls

2. App Preferences

  • Theme selection (Light, Dark, or System Default)
  • Notification visibility settings
  • Parental control PIN (if enabled)
  • Silent schedule settings (if used)

3. Service Status

  • Whether volume monitoring services are active
  • Service configuration preferences

No Personal Information Collected

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Any data that leaves your device

How We Use Your Information

All data stored locally is used solely for:

1. App Functionality

  • Maintaining your volume lock preferences
  • Enforcing volume constraints as configured
  • Preserving your theme and display preferences
  • Managing scheduled silent periods

2. Service Operation

  • Running background services to monitor and control volume
  • Starting services automatically on device boot (if enabled)

Permissions Required

Our App requires the following permissions to function:

Required Permissions

1. MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS

  • Purpose: To control and lock volume levels for different audio streams
  • Why Needed: Core functionality of the app

2. FOREGROUND_SERVICE

  • Purpose: To run background services that monitor volume changes
  • Why Needed: Ensures volume locks remain active even when app is in background

3. FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK

  • Purpose: Required for media playback foreground service type (Android 14+)
  • Why Needed: To maintain volume monitoring service

4. POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+)

  • Purpose: To display persistent notification for active volume lock service
  • Why Needed: Required by Android for foreground services

5. RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED

  • Purpose: To automatically restart volume monitoring services after device reboot
  • Why Needed: Ensures volume locks remain active after device restart

6. ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY

  • Purpose: To manage notification settings and Do Not Disturb mode
  • Why Needed: For advanced notification control features

7. SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM (Android 12+)

  • Purpose: To schedule exact silent periods for scheduled silent mode
  • Why Needed: For precise timing of scheduled silent periods

Permission Usage

  • All permissions are used exclusively for the stated purposes
  • No permissions are used to collect or transmit personal data
  • You can revoke permissions at any time through your device settings

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms
  • Data is stored in:
    • SharedPreferences (for app settings)
    • DataStore (for volume settings and preferences)

Data Security

  • All data remains on your device
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No cloud backup or synchronization

Data Deletion

  • Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data
  • You can clear app data through device settings at any time

Third-Party Services

We do NOT use any third-party services that collect your data.

The App does not integrate with:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage services
  • Social media platforms
  • Payment processors
  • Any external data collection services

Children's Privacy

Our App may be used by parents to control device volume for children. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. All data remains on the device and is not transmitted anywhere.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. Access Your Data

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • You can view and modify settings through the app interface

2. Delete Your Data

  • Uninstall the app to delete all data
  • Clear app data through device settings

3. Control Permissions

  • Grant or revoke permissions through device settings
  • Note: Revoking certain permissions may affect app functionality

4. Stop Services

  • Disable volume locks at any time
  • Stop background services through app settings
