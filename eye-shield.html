Privacy Policy

Eye Guard - Blue Light Filter

AI-powered eye protection with smart blue light filtering and personalized care recommendations.

Welcome to Eye Guard - Blue Light Filter ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your choices. Eye Guard - Blue Light Filter runs fully on-device and does not transmit your personal data to our servers.

By using Eye Guard - Blue Light Filter, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the application.

Information the App Accesses

On-device Preferences (stored locally):

We store your settings locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences, including:

  • Screen dimming level and filter strength
  • Blue light filter mode, color temperature, and presets
  • Advanced filter options (sepia, grayscale, invert, etc.)
  • Theme preference (system/light/dark)
  • Scheduling preferences (enabled/disabled, sunrise/sunset vs. custom times, chosen times)
  • Onboarding status and feature toggles (e.g., auto-adjust)

Optional Coarse Location (on-device only):

If you enable the "sunrise/sunset" scheduling option, the app may access your approximate location (ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) to estimate local sunrise and sunset times. This location is read from the device's last known location if permission is granted. The location is used only to compute times on your device, is not stored by the app, and is not sent to any server.

Sensors (on-device only):

If you enable auto-adjust, the app reads ambient light sensor values in real time to adjust brightness and filter strength. These readings are not stored or transmitted.

We do NOT collect or transmit:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, photos, videos, or files
  • Precise GPS location
  • App usage outside of Eye Guard - Blue Light Filter or browsing history
  • Financial information or payment data
  • Screen content or screenshots
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics, crash reports, or diagnostics to third parties

How We Use Information

  • App functionality: Provide screen dimming and blue light filtering via an on-screen overlay.
  • Personalization: Remember your preferences, presets, and theme.
  • Scheduling: Turn the overlay on/off at times you select or around sunrise/sunset.
  • Notifications: Show a persistent notification that lets you toggle Eye Guard - Blue Light Filter.
  • Performance: Operate the foreground service needed for a reliable overlay.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage:

Your preferences are stored locally using Android SharedPreferences. We do not operate any backend that stores your personal data. If your device backup is enabled, the operating system may include app preferences in device backups per system settings.

Security:

Android provides app sandboxing and OS-level protections. Eye Guard - Blue Light Filter does not implement additional encryption for SharedPreferences because it does not store sensitive personal data. Always keep your device updated and protected.

Permissions We Request and Why

  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW: To display the screen overlay for dimming and filtering.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE: To run the overlay continuously and reliably in the foreground.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+): To show a persistent control notification (optional but recommended).
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: To restore your chosen state and reschedule timers after device restart.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM (Android 12+): To switch the overlay on/off at exact times when scheduling is enabled.
  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION (optional): Only used to estimate sunrise/sunset when you enable that scheduling mode; not stored or transmitted.

Permissions are used solely to deliver core functionality. We do not use them for tracking or advertising.

Third-Party Services

Eye Guard - Blue Light Filter does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or crash reporting SDKs. Distribution and updates are provided by Google Play.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Your Choices

  • Settings: Change any preference within the app at any time.
  • Permissions: Grant or revoke runtime permissions in Android settings.
  • Clear data: Clear app data in system settings to reset to defaults.
  • Uninstall: Remove the app to delete all local data.

Children's Privacy

Eye Guard - Blue Light Filter is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Users

Eye Guard - Blue Light Filter is operated from India. The app processes data locally on your device and does not transmit your personal data to our servers.
