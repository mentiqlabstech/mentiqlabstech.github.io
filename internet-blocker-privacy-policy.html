Privacy Policy for Internet Blocker Pro

Effective Date: January 15, 2025
Last Updated: January 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Mentiq Labs Tech ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Internet Blocker Pro mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 No Personal Data Collection

Internet Blocker Pro does not collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or unique device IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Contact information or address book data
  • Photos, videos, or other media files

1.2 Local Data Only

The app only stores the following data locally on your device:

  • App Blocking Lists: Your selected apps to block internet access
  • App Preferences: Theme selection, blocking settings, and custom configurations
  • VPN Settings: Local VPN configuration for app blocking
  • System Settings: Temporary device settings applied during blocking sessions

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to use. The local data stored on your device is used solely for:

  • Blocking internet access for selected apps
  • Managing VPN service for app blocking
  • Storing your blocking preferences and settings
  • Providing focus mode functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • SharedPreferences: For app settings and preferences
  • Local VPN Service: For app blocking functionality
  • Device Storage: For temporary data and cache files

3.2 Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • All data is stored locally on your device only
  • No data transmission occurs - everything remains on your device
  • VPN service operates locally without external routing
  • Regular security updates and patches are provided

4. App Permissions

Internet Blocker Pro requests the following permissions to provide its functionality:

4.1 Required Permissions

  • VPN Permission: To create local VPN for app blocking
  • Usage Access: To detect and manage app usage
  • System Settings: To modify device settings for focus mode

4.2 Optional Permissions

  • Notification Access: To manage notifications during focus mode
  • Device Admin: For advanced system optimization

5. Third-Party Services

Internet Blocker Pro does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal data. The app operates completely independently and does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Crash reporting services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services
  • External APIs or web services

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose any data to third parties. This includes:

  • No data transmission to external servers
  • No sharing with advertisers or marketing companies
  • No data sharing with government agencies (unless legally required)
  • No sharing with business partners or affiliates

7. Your Rights and Choices

Since we don't collect personal data, most traditional privacy rights don't apply. However, you have the following rights regarding the app:

7.1 Data Control

  • Delete App Data: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • Reset Settings: You can reset all app settings to default
  • Revoke Permissions: You can revoke app permissions at any time
  • Clear Cache: You can clear app cache through device settings

8. Children's Privacy

Internet Blocker Pro is suitable for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's privacy.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
