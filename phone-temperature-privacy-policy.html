Privacy Policy for Phone Temperature Pro

Effective Date: January 15, 2025
Last Updated: January 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Mentiq Labs Tech ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Phone Temperature Pro mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Device Information Only

Phone Temperature Pro collects only essential device information necessary for its core functionality:

  • Battery Information: Battery level, charging status, health, and temperature
  • System Temperature: CPU and device temperature readings
  • App Usage Data: Battery consumption per app for monitoring purposes
  • Device Settings: Temperature unit preferences (Celsius/Fahrenheit)
  • Widget Data: Information displayed on home screen widgets

1.2 No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, including:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contact information or address book data
  • Photos, videos, or other media files
  • Browsing history or internet activity
  • Account credentials or login information

2. How We Use Your Information

The device information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Temperature Monitoring: Display real-time device and battery temperature
  • Battery Analysis: Show battery health, charging status, and consumption rates
  • App Performance: Monitor which apps consume the most battery
  • Widget Updates: Provide live temperature and battery data on home screen widgets
  • Charge Alerts: Notify you when to unplug your charger for battery longevity
  • Settings Management: Remember your temperature unit preferences and theme choices

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage Only

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • SharedPreferences: For app settings and user preferences
  • Local Database: For temperature history and battery statistics
  • Widget Data: For home screen widget information
  • Cache Files: For temporary data and performance optimization

3.2 Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • All data remains on your device - no external transmission
  • Encrypted local storage for sensitive settings
  • No cloud synchronization or backup services
  • Regular security updates and patches

4. App Permissions

Phone Temperature Pro requests the following permissions to provide its functionality:

4.1 Required Permissions

  • Battery Stats: To monitor battery usage and consumption per app
  • System Settings: To access temperature and battery information
  • Notification Access: To provide charge alerts and temperature warnings

4.2 Optional Permissions

  • Widget Access: To display temperature and battery info on home screen
  • Theme Access: To apply dark mode and custom themes

5. Third-Party Services

Phone Temperature Pro does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal data. The app operates independently and does not use:

  • Analytics services or tracking tools
  • Advertising networks or marketing platforms
  • Cloud storage or backup services
  • Social media integrations
  • External APIs or web services
  • Data sharing or synchronization services

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose any data to third parties. This includes:

  • No data transmission to external servers
  • No sharing with advertisers or marketing companies
  • No data sharing with government agencies (unless legally required)
  • No sharing with business partners or affiliates
  • No cloud backup or synchronization

7. Temperature Data Accuracy

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

Temperature readings may vary due to device-specific drivers and hardware limitations. The app provides temperature information based on available system sensors, but accuracy may differ between device models and manufacturers.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

8.1 Data Control

  • Delete App Data: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • Reset Settings: You can reset all app settings to default
  • Revoke Permissions: You can revoke app permissions at any time
  • Clear History: You can clear temperature and battery history
  • Disable Widgets: You can remove home screen widgets

9. Children's Privacy

Phone Temperature Pro is suitable for users of all ages. Since we only collect essential device information and no personal data, there are no special considerations for children's privacy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
