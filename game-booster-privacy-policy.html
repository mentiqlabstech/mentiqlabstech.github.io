Privacy Policy for Game Booster Pro

Introduction

Welcome to Game Booster Pro ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Game Booster Pro (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Game Profiles: When you add games to the app, we store the game name, package name, and your custom optimization settings locally on your device.
  • App Settings: Your preferences for gaming mode, optimization settings, and notification preferences are stored locally.

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device Performance Data: The app monitors and collects performance metrics including:
    • FPS (Frames Per Second) data
    • RAM usage statistics
    • CPU usage information
    • Battery temperature readings
    • Network latency measurements
  • App Usage Information: Information about which games you launch and optimization activities performed.
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as Android version, device model, and system capabilities.

Permissions We Request

Our app requests the following permissions to provide its core functionality:

  • INTERNET: To measure network latency and optimize network settings
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To monitor network connectivity status
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE: To optimize WiFi settings for gaming performance
  • WAKE_LOCK: To prevent the device from sleeping during gaming sessions
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: To run optimization services in the background
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To send notifications about optimization status
  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW: To display performance monitoring overlays
  • PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS: To monitor app usage and optimize performance
  • READ_PHONE_STATE: To access device state information for optimization
  • ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY: To manage notification settings during gaming
  • WRITE_SETTINGS: To modify system settings for optimization

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Performance Optimization: To analyze and optimize your device's gaming performance
  2. Game Management: To organize and launch your games with enhanced settings
  3. Real-time Monitoring: To display performance metrics during gaming sessions
  4. Network Optimization: To improve network connectivity and reduce latency
  5. Background Process Management: To clean up unnecessary processes and free memory
  6. Settings Management: To apply and maintain your preferred optimization settings

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All data collected by the app is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences
  • No personal data is transmitted to external servers
  • Game profiles and optimization settings are stored securely on your device

Data Security

  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
  • All data processing occurs locally on your device
  • We do not collect or store personal identifying information

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third parties. All data collected by the app remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers or shared with any third parties.

Data Retention

  • Performance metrics and optimization records are stored locally and can be deleted by uninstalling the app
  • Game profiles and settings are retained until you manually delete them or uninstall the app
  • You can clear all app data through your device's application settings

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  1. Access: You can view your game profiles and settings within the app
  2. Modification: You can modify or delete game profiles and settings at any time
  3. Deletion: You can delete individual game profiles or uninstall the app to remove all data
  4. Permission Control: You can revoke app permissions through your device settings

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

Third-Party Services

Our app may connect to external servers only for:

  • Network latency testing (ping to Google DNS servers)
  • No personal data is transmitted during these connections

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Data Processing Legal Basis

We process your data based on:

  • Contract Performance: To deliver the promised optimization features
